# music

i make music too! at the moment, i have created 4 albums, an ep and a single. all of this you can find on my bandcamp page. it spans across a wide variety of genres, and i can't guarantee any of it is good! still, i hope you enjoy listening to them.

computer club link

my latest release- an album filled with cool sounds, nice beats and a sad story? i'm quite proud of this one, and it definitely has had the best response so far. i love computers too!!
