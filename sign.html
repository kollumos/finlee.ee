    • ## signpad faq

    ### "what is it for?"

    sign is a language meant to toy around in. it is in no way a good replacement of anything else, and shouldn't be treated as such. instead it offers another look at how programming could work.

    ### "why did you make it?"

    i made sign and signpad as an experiment. before i began work on it i wasn't a very skilled coder, heck i didn't even know what an array was. this project was meant as a way to speed up my coding knowledge, and as a fun challenge. the same turned out true for thego, and we both learned a lot along the way. maybe someday we'll finish it, who knows.

    ### "what can it do?"

    signpad can't do much, really. but it's fun nontheless. it has a restrictive text-based graphics system, with its own fun tileset creatively called signscii. you can make fun little graphics in it, jokes to prank your friends, cellular automata, and maybe a simple game. but that's about it.
